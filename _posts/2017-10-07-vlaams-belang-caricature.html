An old caricature from 2004 illustrating the VB’s name change after a trial condemned the party for racism. The drawing is loosely inspired by the late Willy Vandersteen of Suske en Wiske fame.

The three politicians pictured here are Filip Dewinter, Frank Vanhecke and Gerolf Annemans.
