SARS-COV-2, also know as the novel coronavirus that leads to COVID-19 illness is no joke. Quarantine proved its ability to relieve our healthcare system of some of the pressure it induces. Self-imposed confinement works, as long as everybody participates. This calendar is a symbolic way to convey the idea that each day we spend in confinement makes the virus less damaging.



30 days confinement calendar



15 days quarantine calendar





I drew this for my 10 year old daughter and her classmates. Besides adding a little fun to these strange times, it lets you visualize your time spent in confinement. Kids like to put a face on a situation that may not look so bad even though it may frighten their parents.

If you and your kids are counting the days until you can go outside again freely, this calendar is for you. The virus gets weaker as you cross out/color/tear up each day.

Stay home until the virus is gone.
