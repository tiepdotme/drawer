New Apple Keynote theme template in the Storyboards shop. 2.39:1 ratio (“Scope” sometimes also referred to as 2.40) set on DIN A4 landscape format. Flexible layout: chose your layout, pick a frame number per page (1, 4, 6, 9, 12 or 16), whether you use text or not, page numbering, scenes and shot numbers. Enable guides to use rule of thirds. There are also a number of arrows you can copy and paste into your document to indicate camera move, movement within the shot and character’s eyes direction.

This template will help you put together your feature film Storyboards fast.
