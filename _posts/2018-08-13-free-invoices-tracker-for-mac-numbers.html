If you are a fellow freelancer, feel free to get this free spreadsheet template for Apple Numbers to help keeping track of your assignment invoices and clients payments. The template is set in €, which you can easily change in Numbers. It has a 21% VAT column which you can customize to your country’s percentage, remove or set to 0% if VAT doesn’t apply. The net earning column helps you give you an idea of your actual earnings—check with your accountant which percentage best applies to you. Add or remove rows as you see fit. The template covers a full year.
