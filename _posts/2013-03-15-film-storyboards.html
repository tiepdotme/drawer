FILM STORYBOARDS


Storyboarding is a unique yet simple way to see the whole picture of a project at once. You can use a Storyboards as a visual screenplay, at the writing stage of a film. You can use it to edit before filming: test how a story works in images, organize its sequences rhythm and flow. Storyboards are useful to plan, assess costs and ease communication between production crew members.

This blog is all about storyboards, obviously but also work in process, sketches, doodles and pre-production drawings as well as the occasional filmmaking references and links.
