Here is another paginated Storyboards template for Adobe InDesign (InDesign Markup / IDML format). The frame ratio is 1.33:1 (4:3), a traditional television & computer monitor standard.

The sheet is a Din A4 horizontal (portrait), is ready to use for print and screen presentations. This template lets you write your notes alongside your pictures; simply edit the Copy and Images layers in InDesign. The text is set in Helvetica Regular, 10pt. For images, just drop your boards in the black(or white) Images layer. Toggle on and off the rule of thirds guides in the locked layers to help you frame your compositions.

Fill in the title page, replace the logo and credentials. To edit the footer at once, use the A & C masters.
