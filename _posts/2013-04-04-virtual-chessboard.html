Virtuimagesd animatic frame - second rough, often the best.
The demise of queen Leonor.
D. Juan, King of Castile leave Lisbon promising for vengeance.

Animatic series of drawings done for a cross media project on display at the Battle of Atoleiros Interpretation Centre, Fronteira, Portugal, a project by Maverick ICS

In order to explain visually a complex story of royal intrigue and betrayal, we imagined a virtual chessboard set where each character’s role is crystallised like on a theatre scene. I built a mock up of the room to capture perspective from different angles.
