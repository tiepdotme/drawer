August 9–10, 1877. Big Hole Battle. Col. John Gibbon attempts to attack the Nez Percé camp by surprise. Note the howitzer machine gun, later to be dismantled by the crew under attack. Storyboard for the US Army Cavalry Museum, Fort Riley, Kansas.
